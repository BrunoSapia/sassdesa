The Story

Bruno Stefano Sapia (January 15, 1998) is a DJ and Musical producer based in Campana, Buenos Aires Province.

His career began when he was 13. It started as a hobby, listening to his referent Michael Jackson but when his dad gave him his first mixer, he realized that it was something more than just a

By word of mouth, he began to play music at private parties en events almost every weekend. At the age of 15, he was asked to perform at a dancing club for the first time. There were 2.000 people there. At 17, he was already a regular DJ in another club.

Bruno went on going to secondary school, working as a DJ in large parties of 6.000 to 8.000 people. He also attended several musical courses.

After graduating from High School, he started his studies as a Musical Producer at Capital Federal in Buenos Aires province.

Bruno is currently studying, working as a DJ and producing his own tracks.
